🧩 Professional Summary

My name is Tushar, and I am a Unity developer skilled in both 2D and 3D game development. I have over two year of professional experience and currently work as a freelance Unity developer, collaborating with studios and independent clients to build engaging and high-performance games. Over the past year, I have honed my skills under the guidance of the esteemed Paras Bhalodiya.

I am passionate about creating engaging, high-quality, and innovative gaming experiences that push the boundaries of interactivity and player engagement.
